Time Control

Strategic leadership of a business-critical feature that transformed the platform's value proposition and became a primary driver for enterprise sales.

The Challenge

The platform needed a way to help enterprise customers manage their time-based workflows effectively. Competitors offered basic solutions, but none provided comprehensive time control features.

My Approach

I led the end-to-end development of the Time Control feature, working closely with enterprise customers to understand their pain points. We conducted extensive user research and competitive analysis.

The solution included automated scheduling, time-based notifications, and detailed analytics dashboards.

Result

The feature became a key differentiator for enterprise sales, contributing to a 35% increase in enterprise customer acquisition. Customer satisfaction scores for time management improved by 42%.

Project Details

Role Product Manager
Timeline 12 months
Team 5 Developers, 2 Designers, 1 Data Analyst
Methods User Research, Agile, Data Analytics
