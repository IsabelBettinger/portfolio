Behind every product is a person. Here’s a glimpse into my life outside of work.

I’m someone who genuinely enjoys being on the move. Most days you’ll find me at the gym doing heavy lifting and pushing my limits, with a relaxing sauna session to follow. I enjoy the contrast of a high-energy day with the quiet of a completely unproductive afternoon — it’s all about balance.

I love the underwater world. Diving and snorkeling aren’t just about the quiet — they’re about discovering a completely different environment. There’s something incredibly interesting about exploring an ecosystem that most people never see. It’s the best way I’ve found to fully switch off and stay present.

I travel as often as I can, with Southeast Asia being my favorite region to revisit. I’m a big fan of the food, the atmosphere, and the constant warmth. It’s the one part of the world I never really get tired of — there’s always something new to discover.

My downtime is usually split between books and film. I tend to gravitate toward arthouse cinema and well-written fiction, but specialist literature has its place on my shelf too. I enjoy anything that’s actually worth the time it takes to get through it.

I’m very good at hula hooping. I don’t do it often, but I taught myself via YouTube videos years ago and the skill has stuck with me ever since.
