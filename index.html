Personal

Behind every product is a person. Here's a small glimpse into my life outside of work.

I'm someone who genuinely enjoys being on the move. Most days, you'll find me at the gym doing heavy lifting and pushing my limits with a relaxing sauna session afterwards. I enjoy the contrast of a high-energy day at the gym or on a hike with the quiet of a completely unproductive afternoon. It's all about the balance for me.

I love the underwater world. Diving and snorkeling aren't just about the quiet they're about the discovery of a completely different environment. There is something incredibly interesting about exploring the details of an ecosystem that most people never see, and it's the best way I've found to completely switch off and focus on the present.

I travel as often as I can, with Southeast Asia being my favorite region to revisit. I'm a big fan of the food and the atmosphere, but I mostly love the constant warmth. It's the one part of the world I never really get tired of exploring. There's always something new to see, and the energy of the region is truly inspiring.

My downtime is usually split between books and film. I tend to gravitate toward arthouse cinema and well-written fiction, but you can also find specialist literature in my bookshelf. I enjoy anything that's actually worth the time it takes to get through it.
